    Teaching Objective

    Show the fictional market surrounding a newly approved medicine. The medicine sits at the center, while patients, clinical choice, access, fulfillment, customer engagement, and evidence determine whether it reaches treatment and creates commercial value.

    Final Revision Prompt

    Edit the existing Chapter 1 illustration. Preserve its elegant flat editorial vector style,
white background, restrained pastel blue, muted gold, soft green, and small coral accents.
Simplify the composition drastically.

Make the fictional medicine the unmistakable center of a small external market. Keep one
simple unbranded medicine carton and pill bottle on a clean circular platform in the center.
Around it, show only 4 essential external actors as simplified half-body editorial figures or
compact vignettes, evenly spaced with generous whitespace: 1 patient, 1 specialist HCP, 1

identifying object. Do not include internal company teams.

a loose, open circle around it, clearly belonging to one fictional market. A few subtle curved
ground lines may unify them. No arrows, flowchart, boxes, or concentric infographic rings.

The image should communicate that one newly approved medicine creates a market made of
patients, clinical choice, access, fulfillment, customer engagement, and evidence.
