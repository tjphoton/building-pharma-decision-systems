# Chapter 1: A Medicine, a Market, and the Decisions Between Them

Welcome to Chapter 1! This chapter introduces the fictional pharmaceutical launch scenario that forms the foundation for the entire book.

## What You'll Learn

In this chapter, you'll be introduced to:
- **Roventra**: a newly approved medicine entering a competitive market
- **Key market players**: patients, healthcare providers (HCPs), payers, and specialty pharmacies
- **Core decision challenges**: patient reachability, account prioritization, and outcome measurement
- **The role of decision science**: bridging data and action in pharmaceutical launches
